# Taskologist — Product Naming Research

## Chosen Name

**Taskologist** — task + -ologist. "The science of your recurring tasks."

Memorable, universally understood, works for both household (home maintenance) and IT/ops audiences. No existing app, SaaS product, or software company found with this name (verified March 2026).

## Target Audience

Recurring maintenance task tracking for:

- **Households** — home maintenance (filter changes, HVAC service, smoke alarm batteries, etc.)
- **IT/ops** — recurring operational checks and maintenance procedures
